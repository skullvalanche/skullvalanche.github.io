What the hell am I looking at?

In Return of the King, the 3rd film of the Lord of the Rings trilogy, some movie wizards thought it would be cool to throw in a scene in which our heroes were put upon by an avalanche of skulls. The scene involved quite a lot of CG rendering, was likely $1,000,000+ to make, and ultimately was left out of the theatrical version of the film. It wasn't even in the Tolkein books. It was the brain-child of a Hollywood movie committee.
